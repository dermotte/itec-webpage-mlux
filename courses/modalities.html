Text documents

For all text documents authors have to ensure they are readable for all participants. For non editable text PDF should be preferred, for editable text either Microsoft Word 97-2003 format or the most recent LibreOffice format.

Emails

For emails I recommend to:

  • provide a meaningful subject line
  • send them in plain text (no HTML, RTF, or PDF with the content)
  • send them with a minimum attachement size (e.g. resize images, use the “reduce size” functions of PDF generators and text editors, etc.)
  • follow the netiquette guidelines
